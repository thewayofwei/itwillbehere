I would like to quote a few lines from La Vagulette;
Moi, je suis serai tonjours là.
À voir le monde et sa beauté.
The translation in English would be:
I am and always will be here.
To see the world and its beauty.
These verses have resonated with me. I thought I would still remain to see the future and its glory, but I do not know if this is what I desire.
If I leave this world, I would have not fulfilled my promise of still being here. But, this is what truly will occur and I have decided. It will not really matter,
I find it the best course of action to do so. I have not planned to remain and continue in my ways and now I have seen its light.
